Deep Learning for Ad Targeting

At InMobi I designed deep neural network architectures for predicting mobile app installs and purchases in advertising systems.

Problem

Traditional ad targeting systems relied on gradient boosting models. These models struggled to capture complex interactions between user behavior, device signals, and campaign metadata.

Approach

Designed a neural architecture based on Deep Cross Networks to model high-order feature interactions between user signals and advertising campaign data.

System Scale

  • Training data: billions of ad events
  • Distributed training pipelines
  • Real-time inference for ad auctions

Impact

  • 20-40% improvement in advertiser KPI metrics
  • Improved CPI and CPA performance
  • First neural network deployed in production replacing LightGBM
